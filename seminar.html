生命／邊界：生命政治、後人文、非人

Life at the Threshold：Biopolitical, Posthuman, Nonhuman

The international symposium on Life at the Threshold:Biopolitical,Posthuman,Nonhuman (Oct. 14-15, 2016), will take place in the New Liberal Arts Building, hosted by the Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures, NationalChungHsingUniversity, Taichung, Taiwan.

協辦單位：台灣科技藝術學會
贊助單位：生態學會、文鶴書局、敦煌書局、書林書局、聯方科技會
